Force Diagrams

Earthquake Dataset

This earthquake dataset can be used as a force diagram. Each earthquake would be made as a circle on the graph and would have its relevant data loaded into the circle. Then, the edges can be decided by a certain attribute of the previous earthquake to the next one. For example, you could use the depth as the edge and connect each earthquake by their depth.

Fifa Players Dataset

You could use the Fifa 19 players as a force diagram. Each player in the dataset would be a circle and would have their data loaded in, making them a node. Then, you can make the edges of the nodes represent the difference between age.

U.S. Students Dataset

You can use the U.S. education dataset to represent a force diagram. Each value in the dataset could be a circle with its data loaded into it. Then, you have many options to choose what the edges will represent because there are many differing factors. You could fill in the color of the circle by state, or any other attribute you need to use in the dataset. Then, you could connect the circles by their total revenue, year, or any other attribute in the circle.
